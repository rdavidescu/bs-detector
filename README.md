# BS Detector

A Chrome extension that helps you analyze online content for credibility, manipulation patterns, and evidence quality — using your own AI.

---

## What is this?

BS Detector is not about banning content or detecting AI.

It’s about helping you **think twice before believing or sharing something**.

When something feels off:
- click the extension
- run a quick or deep analysis
- understand what’s really going on

---

## Why this exists

The internet is not broken because of AI.

It’s broken because:
- content is optimized for attention, not truth
- emotional manipulation spreads faster than facts
- confidence is often fake, but convincing

BS Detector adds a small but important thing back:
**friction before belief**

---

## Features

- **Quick Scan** → fast gut check  
- **Standard Review** → balanced, structured analysis  
- **Deep Dive** → detailed, claim-by-claim breakdown  

- Multi-provider support (use your own API keys)
- Works with different AI models (Gemini, OpenRouter, Groq, etc.)
- Transparent scoring system (no black-box “trust me”)
- Clear uncertainty — no fake certainty

---

## How it works

1. You open a webpage
2. Click the extension
3. Choose analysis depth
4. The extension sends the content to your chosen AI model
5. You get a structured report:
   - claims
   - evidence quality
   - manipulation signals
   - BS score (with explanation)

---

## Important notes

- This is **not a truth engine**
- It does **not verify reality fully**
- It evaluates the **content you see**, not the entire world behind it

👉 Scores are estimates, not verdicts  
👉 A deeper investigation can change the outcome
